Features

The game Minecraft by itself is mostly single player and it can be lonely at times. We build a MMORPG oriented community, with features that extend far beyond what is currently being offered by the game, making the experience socially engaging and fun to play!

Core Mechanics

Get the basics right with essential services that provide - global tablist, cross server messaging, cross server teleportation and a sophisticated data controller.

Social Mechanics

Beautiful subdomain name, custom domain to brand your world. Global tablist, global messaging, global teleportation to integrate players into an unified, social gameplay environment with their friends.

Virtually Unlimited Storage

At the heart of Minecraftly is a global multi-petabyte scale storage with high throughput and Max I/O performance. All of that to host all the Minecraft worlds for all players

  • Our storage cluster is built on flash technology to ensure your world loads blazing fast, all the time.
  • A centralized storage cluster helps you teleport to other players' worlds without breaking the gameplay experience, providing the most realistic MMORPG experience possible

Control With Ease

Build better community for yourself and leave the operations to us. Save crucial time keeping spammers and trolls out of your world.

  • Our Anti Spam system is implemented at our edge locations around the world, no action is required from your side.
  • Mute people from talking in your world via /mute command.
  • Kick trolls from your world via /kick command.
  • Ban a troll completely from your world via /ban command
  • We have Google Enterprise grade, machine learning DDoS protection in place, supported by Google Cloud Platform. You don't have to worry about your world being DDoSed at all.

Cross world interactions

Unlike other solutions, where your inventories, XP and saved points are gone when you jump to other players' server/world, we keep all of your data consistent everywhere you go. It makes you feel that you are part of a giant community.

Grow

Acquire friends and followers. Engage and play with people you know and new friends at any time. Take the guesswork out of growing your community.

Economy

Interact with extensive virtual economies with inventory systems, in-game stores, p2p marketplaces, crafting and dynamic distribution of resources.
